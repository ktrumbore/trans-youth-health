Dedicated Support for Trans Youth

Our Mission

accessible care is within reach

The Mazzoni Center is here for you. As a beacon of support in Philadelphia's LGBTQIA+ community, we offer gender-affirming services, counseling, and medical care tailored to your needs.

Our mission is simple: to empower you to be your true, authentic self. The transgender youth campaign is about breaking down barriers and building resilience. We want you to know that you're not alone. Together, we can overcome obstacles and champion your journey to self-discovery and happiness.

the realitIES of trans youth

More than 60,000 Philadelphia residents identify as LGBTQIA+. Despite the community’s prevalence, transgender students are more likely to face pervasive harassment than their peers. Healthcare can ease the struggle.

Transgender youth with access to hormone therapy have lower rates of depression and are at a lower risk for suicide.

Every credible medical organization in the U.S. calls for age-appropriate gender-affirming care for transgender and non-binary people.
